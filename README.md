# Frontier Flat Rate — Own Your AI

**Stop renting AI. Start owning it.**

The AI industry is shifting to subscription models that extract value from developers. Frontier Flat Rate documents the evidence and provides a clear alternative: own your AI infrastructure instead of renting it from vendors who can change your pricing overnight.

## The Problem

- **Microsoft Copilot:** 9x price increase (June 2025). Token billing makes costs unpredictable.
- **Anthropic:** Credit split on June 15, 2026. Third-party tools like OpenClaw directly affected.
- **Google I/O 2026:** Gemini features gated behind $20-250/mo tiers. Hardware locked.
- **GitHub Copilot:** Opaque token billing. No published per-model rates.
- **Gmail:** 15GB → 5GB storage reduction. Your data, their rules.

## The Solution

**Own vs Rent.** Run your own AI agents. One flat rate. No surprise billing. No vendor lock-in. Your data stays yours.
